Duffy and D-O-G – our logo pets

May 20, 2005

Duffy, my precious kitty companion, left this world. He is now with D-O-G, my Forever Canine Companion. During their lives they brought happiness to so many. I love and miss them so much. In the midst of my sadness, I feel solace knowing their legacy will live on through the Forget Me Not logo LaVona Peabody so artistically created. A heartfelt thank you for all the Forget Me Not Animal shelter is doing for all the animals in need of loving homes.

— Dee Dee
