Chrissy 1577

Chrissy is a wonderful young "senior" of 7 who maintains her extreme love of all things BALL RELATED!!! She just adores playing, and is a very sweet and loving girl who appears to be a mix of Golden and Labrador Retriever.

Chrissy loves car rides - everything in life is a fun adventure to this happy gal - and is quite well behaved as a passenger. She is totally housetrained, will play with you as long as you want but not be obnoxious about it when it's time to quit, and will also play with toys on her own. She is very enthusiastic about everything - definitely an optimist, with a big smile and sparkle in her eyes. She is kind of a goofy lunkhead, and thankfully does not jump up on people while expressing her joie d'vivre! She could use some additional leash training to remember that not everyone on the other end of the leash wants to run after that fluffy squirrel. She listens well and takes gentle corrections happily, remembering them next time, which is a wonderful quality.

Chrissy is somewhat like the sweet, loveable, not-too-intellectual but loved-by-all high school cheerleader stereotype. If you've ever wanted a reason to get up in the morning with a smile, Chrissy will be happy to provide that motivation!

Her geriatric panel showed no major concerns, and she is having a couple of lumpy-bumps removed in January, after which she will be ready to head home with you!

She is pretty good with other dogs - can be a little bit bossy at times, but learns the hierarchy pretty fast. She has fostered with one or two other dogs since arriving at the shelter, and all have managed to work out their relationships with each other without too much drama.

Chrissy lived with a cat peacefully in the past, so we think she will adapt fine to a dog-savvy cat if given proper introductions and enough time to get to know each other. She loves all sorts of humans and would enjoy having any type of family situation, from one person to a whole family full of kids.
